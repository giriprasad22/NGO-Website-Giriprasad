About Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation

Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation is a government-registered NGO (Indian Societies Act, 1860) dedicated to breaking the barriers of education in underserved communities.

Our vision is to empower underprivileged children living in slums and marginalized localities through free quality education, life skills, nutrition, and holistic development.

We create safe, nurturing spaces where every child can learn, dream, and thrive.

  • Accessible Learning: Free, nurturing classes for children in Noida and Delhi slums—every session a step toward independence.
  • Life Skills & Support: Personality development, digital literacy, hygiene, nutrition, creative arts, and sports programs for holistic empowerment.
  • Community Uplift: Family and community engagement with women's groups, volunteers, and donors driving change collectively.
  • Sustainable Change: Long-term transformation enabled by advocacy, awareness, and strategic partnerships.
